Frog on a Log in a Foggy Bog

I created an animated environment for my Advanced VFX class.

I used Bifrost Graph node-based programming to generate interesting terrain, wavering grass, and eddying fog.

Lightsaber Duel

I transformed a green screen video of my friend and I into a battle for the fate of the galaxy using DaVinci Resolve/Fusion.

  • Lightsabers tracked and rotoscoped in Fusion
  • Lightning rendered in Maya, post-produced in Fusion
  • Color corrected, garbage masked

Potion Master

Hallowed Night

I independently developed a 2D platformer with Unity "inspired by" my favorite 2D game, Hollow Knight, with all of the following features:
